About Web3

Web3 represents the next evolution of the internet, enabling a decentralized and user-centric web experience. It leverages blockchain technology, smart contracts, and peer-to-peer networks to empower users with greater control over their data,enhanced security, and seamless interactions with decentralized applications (dApps).
